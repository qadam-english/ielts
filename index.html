About Qadam English Course

Qadam English is a professional IELTS preparation course designed to help students achieve high band scores. Our program focuses on developing all four IELTS skills: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking. We provide structured lessons, real exam strategies, and personalized feedback to support every learner.

Exam Format

Listening

Understanding spoken English (40 mins).

Reading

Academic and general texts (60 mins).

Writing

Organizing ideas clearly (60 mins).

Speaking

Face-to-face interview (11-14 mins).
