About Qadam English Course

Qadam English is a professional IELTS preparation course designed to help students achieve high band scores. Our program focuses on developing all four IELTS skills: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

What is IELTS?

IELTS is a globally recognized English proficiency exam required for studying abroad and immigration.

Exam Format

Listening

Understanding spoken English (40 mins).

Reading

Academic and general texts (60 mins).

Writing

Organizing ideas clearly (60 mins).
