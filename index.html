Reading Passage: The Future of AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the way we live and work. From self-driving cars to advanced medical diagnostics, AI's potential seems limitless. However, many experts express concerns about ethical implications and job displacement. Research suggests that while AI may replace some manual tasks, it will also create new roles that require human creativity and emotional intelligence. Understanding this balance is crucial for future development.

1. According to the text, what is one major benefit of AI?

A) It removes all ethical concerns.
B) Advanced medical diagnostics.
C) Total elimination of job displacement.
D) It only affects manual labor roles.

2. What skills will be required for the new roles created by AI?

A) Basic mechanical skills.
B) Ability to work without breaks.
C) Human creativity and emotional intelligence.
D) Superior speed in data entry.

3. What is the main concern expressed by experts regarding AI?

A) Lack of speed.
B) Ethical implications and jobs.
C) Cost of implementation.
D) Limited diagnostic abilities.

4. How does AI impact manual tasks according to the passage?

A) It has no impact on them.
B) It makes them harder.
C) It replaces some of them.
D) It increases the need for them.

5. What is considered "crucial" for future development?

A) Ignoring the balance between tasks.
B) Understanding the balance of AI and human roles.
C) Accelerating job displacement.
D) Disabling diagnostic tools.
